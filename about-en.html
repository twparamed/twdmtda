About TWDMTDA

Taiwan Disaster Medical Development Association

Founded with the mission of establishing a nationwide operational system for disaster medical teams and safeguarding public safety during disasters.

Building a Resilient Defense Line

Officially registered with the Ministry of the Interior in 2017, TWDMTDA is a non-profit organization. We believe that in the critical moments following a disaster, mutual aid and professional medical response are essential to saving lives.

Certified Training

Recognized by MOHW as an EMT-II training organization.

Official Registry

Registered disaster rescue organization with the Taoyuan City Government.

Years of Field Experience

Over the past eight years, we have conducted numerous training programs and participated in international missions.
